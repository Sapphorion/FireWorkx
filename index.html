About Us

With over 15 years of experience in sprinkler installation and maintenance, we can provide 24 hour service in all areas of fire protection, including all hand-held fire protection equipment. Fully approved by SACAS and compliant with SAQCC 1475, we pride ourselves on delivering reliable, high-quality solutions that meet the highest safety and regulatory requirements. Our commitment to excellence ensures peace of mind for our clients across a wide range of industries across South Africa. With our committed team we handle commercial and residential properties, industrial sites, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and more. Our services include fire extinguisher servicing and maintenance, fire sprinkler system installation and maintenance, fire risk assessments, emergency lighting installation and maintenance, fire alarm system installation and maintenance, and fire safety training for staff.
