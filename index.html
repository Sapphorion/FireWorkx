About

With over 15 years of experience in sprinkler installation and maintenance, we can provide 24 hour service in all areas of fire protection including all hand-held fire protection equipment. Fully approved by SACAS and compliant with SAQCC 1475, we pride ourselves on delivering reliable, high-quality solutions that meet the highest safety and regulatory requirements. Our commitment to excellence ensures peace of mind for our clients across a wide range of industries across South Africa.
